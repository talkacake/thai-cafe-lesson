Question ideas (WRONG answers)

  1. Are you a student?

    Examples: “Yes, I am.”, “No, I don’t have a job.”

  2. What do you wear today?

    วันนี้ใส่อะไร

    Examples: “A banana suit”, “A crown”

    No, I don’t wear anything.

    It makes me sleep well.

    You can burn more calories without clothes.

  3. What is your favourite food?

    Examples: “Computers”, “Blue chairs”

    Cat soup because I can find cats everywhere.

  4. What is your hobby?

    I’m dating Jackson Wang.

  5. What do you do on the weekend?

    Examples: “I fly to the moon.”, “I talk to penguins.”

  6. What do you do with a pencil?

    ทำอะไรกับดินสอ

    Pencil salad.

Extension activity: “The Hot Seat”

  1. The teacher puts one student in the hot seat. ครูให้นักเรียนคนนึงนั่งที่ร้อน
  2. The teacher shares a funny image on the screen for everyone to see. ครูแชร์รูปตลกๆให้ทุกคนดู
  3. The teacher randomly picks another student to ask a question. ครูสุ่มเลือกนักเรียนอีกคนมาถามคำถาม
  4. The questioner asks one question (e.g., “What is he doing?”). คนถามหนึ่งคำถาม เช่น เขาทำอะไร
  5. The one in the hot seat must immediately give a funny, wrong answer (e.g., “He is eating a banana!”). ให้คำตอบที่ตลกและผิดทันที เช่น เขากินกล้วย
  6. Then choose a new student for the hot seat and a new questioner.
